What's for Dinner Help

The purpose of What's for dinner is to randomly choose a Boulder restuarnt between eight restuarnts. It is currently programmed for fast casual resturants and to chooses between Illegal Pete's, Rincon Argentino, Zoe Ma Ma, Snarfs, Noodles & Company, Cafe Mexicali, Garbonzo, and Boss Lady Pizza.

There is a color wheel with these resturaunts listed on them. The wheel can be spun by shaking the device or by tapping the 'Spin!' button. The wheel then spins for a random amount of time and then updates the user with the restuarant corresponding to what part of the wheel was under the selector when it stopped spinning.
